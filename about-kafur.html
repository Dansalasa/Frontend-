Babban Bikin Kafur

Kafur Festival Connect babban biki ne na al’adu da haɗin kai, wanda aka shirya domin haɗa matasa, manya da baki daga ko’ina. Wannan biki ba irin wanda aka taɓa yi bane a tarihin Kafur.

A cikin bikin za a samu nishaɗi, al’adu, wasanni, tattaunawa da kuma haɗin kai tsakanin al’umma.
